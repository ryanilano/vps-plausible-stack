# 🥭 Mango's Server Adventure — A Manga Explainer

> A 6-panel educational manga that explains **vps-plausible-stack** to a total beginner.
> Each panel has: the **story beat**, the **on-page text/dialogue**, and a ready-to-paste
> **🎨 Image Prompt** you can drop into ChatGPT / DALL·E / Midjourney / Gemini.

**Art direction (paste this once at the top of any image session):**
> Clean, friendly black-and-white manga / shōnen style with soft screentones and a single
> spot color (leafy green). Cute round mascot characters. Clear, readable speech bubbles.
> Wholesome "explainer comic" energy, not action-violent.

**The cast:**
- **🥭 Mango** — an eager round mango mascot building their first website. The reader stand-in.
- **🛡️ Caddy** — a calm knight/guard character with a padlock shield. The front door.
- **📊 Sensei Plausible** — a wise, friendly teacher who shows charts. The dashboard.
- **🕵️ G. Analytics** — a sneaky suited spy mascot (the thing we're replacing).

---

## Panel 1 — The Problem

**Beat:** Mango is proud of a new website but uneasy about who's watching the visitors.

**Text:**
- Mango: *"My website is live! But… how do I know who visits — without spying on them?"*
- Caption (top): *Every site wants stats. Most pay for them with their visitors' privacy.*

> 🎨 **Image Prompt:** Manga panel, black-and-white with green spot color. A cute round
> mango mascot with little arms stands proudly in front of a glowing laptop showing a simple
> website. Mango looks slightly worried, sweat-drop. Behind, a shadowy spy in a suit (labeled
> "G. ANALYTICS") peeks from the corner with binoculars. Speech bubble from Mango: "How do I
> know who visits — without spying on them?" Wholesome explainer-comic style.

## How to use this file

1. Paste the **Art direction** block into your image generator first.
2. Then paste each **🎨 Image Prompt** one at a time to get all 6 panels.
3. Add the **Text / dialogue** into the speech bubbles yourself (or ask the model to include it).
4. Stack the 6 images top-to-bottom and you've got a complete explainer manga. 🥭
